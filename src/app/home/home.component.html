Don’t trust the hype?

We at Doomie’s believe in offering the comfort foods that you grew up with. That’s why we decided that the next restaurant we opened had to offer the food that we grew up with. And in the heart of Los Angeles, we knew we had to offer Mexican food.

Doomie’s was started as a place to get vegan food that wasn’t filled with brown rice and seasonal veggies, wrapped in whole wheat tortillas, and served on a plate of kale.

This is vegan food that is unapologetic about how greasy it is. It isn’t healthy and was never intended to be. Doomie’s is the place to go when your partner is vegan, and you’re not, when you’re hungover and you need a plate covered in grease and cheese, for when you need a reminder of what vegan food can be.
